### The Team
SlapCap is run by a team of developers and designers, working to make this the next big hit! We're slowly but steadily bringing SlapCap to your very own school! At the moment, we're closed-source while we get going, but we'll be open-source in no time!

### Contribute
While SlapCap is closed-source, we're inviting developers and designers to our organization and to the repository- and anybody can apply!
